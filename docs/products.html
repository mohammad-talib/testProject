Sterile Lyophilized Powder for Injection

A lyophilizer executes a water removal process typically used to preserve perishable materials, to extend shelf life or make the material more convenient for transport.

Lyophilizer work by freezing the material, then reducing the pressure and adding heat to allow the frozen water in the material to sublimate.

Sterile Solutions for injection

The injectables vial production line is composed of washing machine, sterilizer, filling and stoppering machine, and capping machine, inspection, labelling machines, vial sterile filling line.

Pharmaceuticals Sterile Injectable vial filling machine production line is mainly used for Vial washing, sterilizing, filling, stoppering and capping for injection vials ranging from 1-60ml in pharmaceutical industries under aseptic conditions.

The Aseptic vial washing filling stoppering capping, and labelling line consists of washing machine, hot air circulation drying sterilization tunnel, Servo vial filling stoppering machine, and capping machine, each machine can be used in combination or in single line. Pharmaceutical Sterile injectable projects can be done according to international standard like EU cGMP, USA FDA, WHO GMP.
