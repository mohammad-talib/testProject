About Us

TARoox is committed to be the leading provider of life-saving medications in the Middle East and North Africa. Cure Pharma aims to manufacture pharmaceutical products that meet the expected needs in terms of quality, efficacy and safety in accordance with national laws and regulations and the relevant international guidelines to meet the expectations of customers and healthcare professionals.

TARoox aims to raise the efficiency of drug treatment through

* Providing medicines that are characterized by Efficacy, Quality & Safety, in accordance with local and international guidelines.

* Meeting the needs of the local market in Iraq and the whole region for these Hi-tech medicines.

* An economic price that matches the average citizen's income.

Our vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading science-driven generic pharmaceutical manufacturer of the highest quality and affordable antineoplastic and speciality products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the overall health-related quality of life of our patients by delivering efficacious, safe, quality and cost-effective products, in compliance with technical, legal requirements.
